Projects

Using weak electrical brain stimulation to improve memory in older adults
This summer I will conduct a pilot study using gamma frequency tACS on healthy adults over the age of 50 to determine if the stimulation improves their memory encoding and sleep architecture. This project is funded by the Foundation for Hope and is IRB approved.
Discovering which brain waves are used to hold items in memory
For the past 8 months, I've been collecting EEG, fMRI, and TMS data for a study designed by Dr. Justin Riddle which investigates the role of theta and alpha oscillations in the cognitive control of working memory. This project uses peak power frequency individualization as well as fMRI guided TMS.
Catching when participants cheat on cognitive psychology tasks
I performed an independent study in the spring semester of 2023 to analyze behavioral data from a visual working memory task for evidence of bias. I am preparing the manuscript of the results of an analysis of this bias strategy that ran agianst what we instructed participants to do.
Mental illness destigmatization
I work with a non-profit organization called the TEACH initiative to develop and present educational and destigmatizing presentations on mental illness to high schoolers. We take a science-based and harm reduction-centric approach to education.

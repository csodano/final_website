About

I want to improve the lives of people with severe mental illness through research. I draw from my lived experience with bipolar disorder to focus on answering those questions that matter most to patients. Particularly, I want to develop new and improve existing nonpharmacological treatments. At the moment. I am exploring non-invasive brain stimulation as a possible treatment for illnesses like depression, anxiety. and schizophrenia.

I also care deeply about science communication and empowering the public to make informed decisions about their psychiatric care. as well as to reduce harm of psychiatric crises in adolescents and youth.
